Welcome to LITTLE FEEDING FRENZY!
You are a small hungry fish in an ocean 
full of big fish. The only way to survive is 
to gobble up the little guys quick and 
crafty to avoid becoming a meal yourself 
until you can challenge even the most 
dangerous predators of the deep!
You can move your fish with the mouse.
If you want to end game during playing, 
press "Enter" to over this game and see 
your scores!
